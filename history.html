el table
El Table has been a part of the
Wellesley community since 1904.


Originally located in College Hall, El Table began as a trading post called the Campus Exchange. It got its nickname by its convenient location next to the elevator - hence, the 'el' table. When Green Hall was built in 1931, El Table relocated to its current space in the basement of Founders Hall.

Now, El Table serves coffee, sandwiches, and snacks and provides a community space for students and professors. Check out a documentary made by El Tabler Adrienne Ogle '15 about our history!
