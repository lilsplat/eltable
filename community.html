Bring your community event to El Table!
Host your next performance, lecture, or workshop at El Table! El Table seeks to be a community space open to all - for more details on hosting events, contact our general manager.

Past events hosted at El Table include:
  • Wellesley Review release party
  • A capella performances
  • Dead Serious improv shows
  • Spoken word poetry
  • 24-hour Shakespeare
  • Faculty meetings
  • Classes
  • Org meetings

To see more events, please visit our Event page. If you are interested in catering an event in a different space, please visit our Catering page.
